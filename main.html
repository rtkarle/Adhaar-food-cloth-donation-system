About Us

Aadhar was founded in 2025 with a mission to treat food and essentials as dignified resources, not charity. We work closely with grassroots partners to ensure needs are met and communities are empowered.

  • Community-driven distribution
  • Disaster relief & rehabilitation
  • Menstrual health & dignity programs
  • Education supply drives
How we work
We collect, sort and channel resources through local partners, ensuring accountability and long-term benefits.
