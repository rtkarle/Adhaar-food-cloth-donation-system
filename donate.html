Why Your Donation Matters

🍲 Reduce Food Waste

Large quantities of cooked food are wasted daily while many sleep hungry. Your donation helps convert surplus into timely meals.

👕 Restore Dignity

Clean, usable clothing gives people confidence, warmth, and dignity — not charity.

🌱 Sustainable Impact

We believe in responsible redistribution, reducing landfill waste and supporting communities ethically.

What We Accept

Clear guidelines help us ensure dignity, safety, and real impact.

✔ Accepted Items

  • 🍱 Fresh cooked food (same day)
  • 🥫 Properly packed food
  • 👕 Clean & wearable clothes
  • 🧥 Seasonal essentials

✖ Not Accepted

  • ⛔ Expired or stale food
  • 🦠 Unhygienic items
  • 🧻 Torn or unusable clothes
  • ⚠️ Unsafe materials
